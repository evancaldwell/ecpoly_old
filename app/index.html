My name is Evan Caldwell. I live and work on the edge. The web is my playground and code is my third language (after Japanese).

Take a look around. If you are looking to hire me go ahead and tap/click the contact icon. If you are looking to work with mecheck out my projects. If you are one of those strange people who just want to know more about me, well I made a page just for you, hit up my bio.

When I said playground I wasn't kidding, there are plenty of little extras on my site.. if you look hard enough.

About Me

Who I am

I am a web developer.. to the core. I spent 10 years in business before life guided me back to my true calling. I loved business and still actively participate in business groups/activities but ultimately that compiler in my brain led me back to development.

I have a diverse background and have worked in much of the US as well as internationally (I still get cravings for actual Asian food). These experiences give me a well rounded perspective and the ability to see the big picture on any project. Additionally, it has helped me build my founding principles:

  • Integrity above all else
  • Outwork.. everyone
  • Always push the boundries of technology
  • Never stop learning

I’m devoted to the end product on any endevor and consider each product as a reflection on me as a developer. My business experience puts the client first but I feel an obligation to honestly and openly communicate with those I work with.

What I do

I build custom websites, web applications, web components and internet awesomeness. I recogninze that I’m not the right peg for every hole. If you have a huge project, I’m not your guy. If you have a shoestring budget, I’m not your guy. If you think that you want “just a few simple changes”, I’m not your guy. If on the other hand you want a site that didn’t start with someone elses theme, that I can do. If you want to do something next level with your application, I’m in. If you enjoy a collaborative, brain stormy, living bit of technology, let’s get it rolling. If you have a T-shirt that uses the words “datahead”, “server-side” or has a reference with Japan-glish in it, we’ll probably be good friends.

Other stuff

Webdev is not the only interest in my life, although it does pull a Borg and assimalate everything else once in a while. I love soccer, rock climbing, skiing and most other things that involve some degree of adrenaline enducing activity. I spend time laughing with/at my wife and kids, making great food - I’m not snooty, just adventurous - and occasionally get back to some manly work like fixing stuff, digging holes, branding cows and wrestling crocodiles. Ok, the last one is not true.

I try to express my true self in my work. It is a sum of me in digital.
